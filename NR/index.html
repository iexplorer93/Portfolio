About the collection

Neon Retribution is a collection consisting of unique NFTs that are waiting for their buyer. You can buy, sell, trade or gift your heroes on the OpenSea platform. You can also use your heroes to participate in various activities, games and events that we will organize for our community.

The merger of digital corporations and cyberspace has given birth to virtual cities in neon lights. Here the forces of control and power arose, calling forth legendary heroes. The "Neon Judge" judged abusers of power, paving the way through the digital boulevards. He was joined by cyber heroes, a symbol of resistance and the fight for virtual rights. Battling digital tyrants, they changed the course of history, creating a saga of power and justice in the neon revolution - hope for a brighter future in the fire of neon war.

Crypto Game

We are working on creating an interesting game related to our NFT collection that will attract people. It will be a card battle style game where you can use your NFT heroes to battle other players or the computer. Each hero will have his own powers and abilities, which will depend on his attributes and style. You can improve your heroes, collect new ones and create your own decks. The game will be available on our website after we sell 100% of our NFT heroes. This is one of our roadmap points, which you can see in the "Roadmap" section. We will keep you informed of our progress and provide you with news and updates on the game. We hope you enjoy our game and our NFT collection.
